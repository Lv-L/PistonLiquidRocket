# Requirements
- Bi-propellant liquid rocket
- Can be used for an L2 rocket (airframe integration, 640-5120 Ns)
- Satisfy [Tripoli bi-propellant liquid motors requirements](https://tripoli.org/content.aspx?page_id=5&club_id=795696&item_id=126571&)
- Cost effective (target $3,000)
- Simple (can be designed, manufactured and tested in <1-2 years)
